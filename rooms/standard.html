Accessible Standard Staterooms: Comfort & Convenience at Sea

Our Accessible Standard Staterooms are thoughtfully designed to provide comfort, safety, and accessibility without compromising on your cruise experience. We believe that every guest deserves a relaxing and barrier-free environment, and our standard rooms reflect this commitment.

Key Accessible Amenities

Spacious Layout:
Generously sized to allow easy maneuvering for wheelchairs and mobility aids.
Accessible Bathroom:
Features a roll-in shower or transfer shower with a grab bar, shower seat, and adjustable showerhead.
Lowered Features:
Accessible closet rods, light switches, and thermostats within easy reach.
Emergency Systems:
Visual and auditory emergency alarms for your safety.
Door Widths:
All doors are a minimum of 32 inches wide for comfortable passage.

Standard Stateroom Features

Size:
Approximately 220 sq. ft.
Bedding:
Choice of twin or queen-sized bed with comfortable linens.
View Options:
Interior, Ocean View (with a porthole), or Balcony options available.
Entertainment:
Flat-screen TV with closed captioning options.
Storage:
Ample storage space for personal belongings.
