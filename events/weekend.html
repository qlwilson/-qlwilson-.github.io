Weekend Events: Accessible Adventures & Unforgettable Fun!

At Cripple Cruise Lines, we believe every moment onboard should be an opportunity for joy and connection. Our weekend events are specially curated to be fully accessible, inclusive, and engaging for all our guests, their families, and caregivers. Get ready for a weekend packed with entertainment, relaxation, and memorable experiences!

Friday Night Fun

Welcome Aboard Mixer (7:00 PM - 8:30 PM, Main Lounge):
Kick off your cruise with a vibrant welcome party! Enjoy live music with wheelchair-accessible dance floor space, complimentary accessible mocktails, and opportunities to meet fellow travelers. Our staff will be on hand to assist.
Inclusive Game Night (9:00 PM - 10:30 PM, Games Room):
Featuring a variety of adaptive board games, accessible video games, and trivia challenges. All games are selected to accommodate different cognitive and physical abilities.

Saturday - Day of Discovery

Adaptive Yoga & Stretching (9:00 AM - 10:00 AM, Sun Deck):
Start your day with a refreshing session of adaptive yoga and stretching, led by certified instructors. Chairs and mats provided, with modifications for all mobility levels.
Sensory Art Workshop (11:00 AM - 12:30 PM, Art Studio):
Engage your senses in a creative art session using various textures, sounds, and tactile materials. Wheelchair-accessible workstations and adaptive art tools are available.
Accessible Swimming & Water Games (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM, Main Pool Deck):
Enjoy our pool with a zero-entry ramp and accessible lift. Participate in fun water games or simply relax by the poolside. Lifeguards and trained staff are always present.

Saturday Night Extravaganza

"Stars of the Sea" Talent Show (7:30 PM - 9:00 PM, Grand Theater):
Showcase your talents or cheer on your fellow cruisers! Our stage is fully ramp-accessible with assistive listening devices available.
Silent Disco (9:30 PM - 11:00 PM, Sky Lounge):
Grab a pair of headphones and choose your groove! This unique experience allows everyone to enjoy music at their preferred volume, with plenty of space for mobility aids.

Sunday - Relaxation & Farewell

Accessible Spa & Wellness Hour (10:00 AM - 11:30 AM, Serenity Spa):
Indulge in relaxing mini-massages, aromatherapy, and quiet sensory spaces. Our spa facilities are fully accessible, including changing rooms and treatment areas.
Farewell Brunch (12:00 PM - 1:30 PM, Main Dining Room):
Enjoy a delicious farewell brunch with an array of accessible buffet options and table service.
"Memories Made" Photo Booth (All Day, Atrium):
Capture your favorite cruise moments with fun props and a wheelchair-friendly photo booth.
